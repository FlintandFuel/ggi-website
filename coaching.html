The Programme

The YOU+™
Framework.

Most executives are technically excellent. The ceiling they hit is rarely about knowledge. It is about presence: how they are read in a room, how they communicate under pressure, and whether others follow them with conviction or compliance.

YOU+ is a coaching framework built around a single question: what needs to shift for this leader to reach their next level of impact?

Mark identifies development areas quickly and creates an environment where real change happens — not just in theory, but in practice, in the room, with the people that matter.

Format
1:1 Executive Coaching

Individual sessions tailored to the executive's specific development priorities, pace and context.

Format
Group Coaching Programmes

Cohort-based programmes for leadership teams — building shared presence, communication standards and peer accountability.

Delivery
In-person and Virtual

Delivered globally. Mark has worked with executives across Europe, the Americas, Asia and Africa.

What We Work On

Five dimensions
of executive impact.

01

Leadership Presence

How you enter a room. How you hold attention. The authority you carry when it matters most and the credibility you leave behind when you are gone.

02

Communication and Presentation

Speaking with clarity, conviction and economy. Presenting to boards, media, government and global audiences without hedging or over-explaining.

03

Stakeholder Management

Reading the room correctly. Managing upwards, sideways and down with the same calibration. Building the relationships that make strategy possible.

04

Team Engagement and Style

The way you lead your direct team. How your style lands with different people. The gap, if any, between what you intend and what others experience.

05

Leadership Brand

How you are known. The professional identity you hold inside your organisation and across your industry. The reputation you are building — deliberately or by default.
